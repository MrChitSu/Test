The Great Day and The Great Happy

November 26 / Night

I heard a happy word from my dear Mama Kay Thwe. He made a promise, and so did I. These promises were made by Telegram. These words were recorded with a screenshot photo.

I will always remember the days when I told my mother that I had won the first poetry competition in the township in seventh grade, and the days when I saw her happy smile and the days when I heard the promise of Mama Kay Thwe were the most memorable days of my life. I am also grateful for the destiny that created the bond with my loved one for life. I will cherish it as much as I do.
